About Me

I'm Blake, a designer here in San Francisco. I love designing and coding. I'm so excited that I get to spend my career making functional, beautiful things for the modern web & mobile environments. I have been responsible for leading and producing UI/UX & Visual design for a number of products that millions of people use daily.


An excellent verbal, written and visual communicator, I thrive in collaborative, creative environments where I can focus on improving the intersection of humanity and technology. I will constantly push the limits of technical innovation. I'm an avid listener and enjoy observing real world users interacting with my designs.


A substantial part of my life is dedicated to continuously learning new things and finding ways to improve and evolve myself, my skills and my outlook on life and technology. I am continuously humbled and inspired by the amazing things being designed and built all around me.
